ABOUT THE PRODUCT :

It is a website made purely with the help of HTML and CSS where a user can give out his/her details and get their resume ready as a output in the form of PDF.


How to use this? :


‣Use the given button to redirect to main webpage where you can create your resume
‣Now the webpage opens.
‣Just hover over and click on the fields provided there.
‣Now enter your details as per your needs.
‣After filling all information click on the "Save Your Resume" buttom.
‣It opens a new popup window.
‣change destination to "Save as pdf" and enable Background Graphics Option.
‣Click the bottom Save Button.
‣Finally your resume have been saved in your device.S
